02

Lease Mechanics

A lease is split into two primary components: Depreciation and Rent Charge. You are paying for the value the car loses during your possession, plus the cost of borrowing the capital.

03

Framework

Depreciation

  • The difference between MSRP and Residual Value.
  • The primary cost of a lease.
  • Lower residuals mean higher monthly payments.

Money Factor

  • The interest rate expressed as a decimal.
  • Multiply by 2400 to get the APR equivalent.
  • Paid on the sum of MSRP and Residual Value.

Residual

  • The projected value of the car at the end of the lease.
  • Set by the manufacturer.
  • Highly influential on the monthly payment.
