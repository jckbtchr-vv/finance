Framework

The Expenditure

  • The money leaving your account today.
  • Could be a purchase, subscription, or lifestyle choice.
  • The starting point for calculating opportunity cost.

The Return

  • Historical S&P 500 average: ~10% annually.
  • Conservative estimate: 7-8% after inflation.
  • Compounds year over year.

The Time

  • The multiplier that makes compound interest powerful.
  • 10 years doubles money at 7%.
  • Time in the market beats timing the market.
