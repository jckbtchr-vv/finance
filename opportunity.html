The Habit

  • $7/day coffee = $2,555/year.
  • $15/day lunch = $5,475/year.
  • $100/month subscriptions = $1,200/year.

  • Earlier contributions grow the most.
  • 8% return doubles money in 9 years.
  • Time is the multiplier.

  • Every habit is a trade-off.
