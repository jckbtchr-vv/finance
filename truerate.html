Framework

Hidden Time

  • Commute isn't free — it's unpaid work time.
  • Getting ready for work is work.
  • Decompressing after work is recovery from work.
  • Checking emails at night counts.

Hidden Costs

  • Work clothes you wouldn't buy otherwise.
  • Lunches, coffees, happy hours.
  • Gas, parking, transit passes.
  • The "convenience" tax of being too tired to cook.

The Real Comparison

  • Use TRUE rate to evaluate job offers.
  • Remote job at $70k may beat office job at $85k.
  • Time is the scarcest resource.
  • Optimize for true hourly, not gross salary.
