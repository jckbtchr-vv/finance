Framework

Buying Costs

  • Down payment (opportunity cost).
  • Mortgage interest payments.
  • Property taxes, insurance, maintenance.
  • Transaction costs when selling.

Buying Benefits

  • Equity buildup with each payment.
  • Home appreciation over time.
  • Fixed housing costs (mostly).
  • Tax deductions on mortgage interest.

Renting Trade-offs

  • No equity, but no risk of depreciation.
  • Down payment can be invested elsewhere.
  • Flexibility to move easily.
  • No maintenance costs or surprises.
