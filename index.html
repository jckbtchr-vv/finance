02

Revenue Patterns

Different business models create different revenue patterns. Understanding these patterns helps you identify opportunities and risks.

RECURRING

Subscriptions, memberships. Predictable, compounds over time. Netflix, Spotify, SaaS.

TRANSACTIONAL

One-time purchases. Must constantly acquire new customers. Retail, e-commerce.

PLATFORM

Take a cut of transactions. Value grows with network size. Apple App Store, Airbnb.

ATTENTION

Monetize eyeballs through ads. Free product, sell the audience. Google, Meta, YouTube.

03

Framework

Diversification

  • Multiple revenue streams reduce risk.
  • Apple went from 80% iPhone to 50%.
  • Amazon's AWS subsidizes retail.
  • The best businesses have multiple engines.

Margin Matters

  • Revenue is vanity, profit is sanity.
  • Software has 80%+ margins.
  • Retail often sub-5%.
  • High revenue, low margin = fragile.

Moats

  • Recurring revenue = customer loyalty.
  • Network effects compound value.
  • Switching costs lock in users.
  • Brand is the ultimate moat.
