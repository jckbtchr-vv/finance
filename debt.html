Framework

Snowball Method

  • Pay minimums on everything.
  • Extra goes to smallest balance.
  • Quick wins = motivation.
  • Psychology over math.

Avalanche Method

  • Pay minimums on everything.
  • Extra goes to highest interest rate.
  • Mathematically optimal.
  • Saves the most money.

The Truth

  • Both work if you stick with them.
  • The best method is the one you'll do.
  • Consistency beats optimization.
  • Any extra payment accelerates freedom.
