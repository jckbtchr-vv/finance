Framework

The Base Effect

  • Future raises are percentages of current salary.
  • $80k + 3% = $2,400 raise next year.
  • $75k + 3% = $2,250 raise next year.
  • The gap compounds forever.

The Retirement Multiplier

  • Higher salary = higher 401k match.
  • That extra match compounds for decades.
  • $200 more per month invested for 30 years = ~$240k.
  • Free money multiplied by time.

The Job Hop Effect

  • New jobs often offer 10-20% bumps.
  • That bump is based on your current salary.
  • Starting higher means hopping higher.
  • Every negotiation sets your future floor.
